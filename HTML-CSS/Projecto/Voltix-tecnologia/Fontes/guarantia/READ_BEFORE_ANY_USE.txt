Guarantia is a rustic script typeface with a warm, grounded feel, inspired by traditional lettering and hand-painted signs. It carries a sense of heritage without feeling stiff, combining softness with confident strokes.

The letters flow with an easy rhythm, slightly irregular in a way that feels natural and human. Rounded forms meet firm terminals, giving the script a steady presence while keeping a friendly tone. Guarantia feels at home in environments where authenticity matters, where textures, materials, and craftsmanship are part of the story.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Guarantia was drawn with a focus on balance rather than perfection. Subtle variations in weight and curve give the script its character, making it feel written rather than constructed. The goal was to create a typeface that feels reliable and familiar, like something that has been around for a long time and earned its place.
